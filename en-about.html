About This Website

Sagabai Map is a sightseeing map website created to rediscover the true charm of Saga City, Saga Prefecture. While Saga Prefecture sometimes receives tough evaluations in regional attractiveness rankings, those of us living here know that it is full of moving scenery and experiences beyond famous spots like the "Yoshinogari Ruins." The most unique feature of this site is that it only features spots that our members have actually visited and experienced themselves. We provide a "local's perspective" on the best things to see, activities created by nature, and authentic experiences that you can only find in Saga. We designed this site to be simple and easy to use for both first-time visitors and those who want to know more about Saga. We hope that Sagabai Map will be a starting point for making your trip to Saga City even more special and fulfilling.
