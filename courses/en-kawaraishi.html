Kawaraishi to Ondodai Bridge Running Course

A running course from Kawaraishi Station to Ondodai Bridge.

Course Basic Information

  • Distance: About 8.1km
  • Surface: Asphalt
  • Elevation: High
  • Start Point: Kawaraishi Station

Course Description

A running course from Kawaraishi Station to Ondodai Bridge. Running along the coast, you can feel the Seto Inland Sea close by, and the open scenery continues. The street in the city area is relatively flat and easy to run on, making it suitable for pace runs or relaxed runs. As you approach Ondodai Bridge, the scenery changes significantly, and you can enjoy a powerful view of the bridge and sea. Kure's sea and city atmosphere can be felt while running, making this course recommended for both local runners and tourists.

Recommended Points

  • ✔ Feel the history of Kure
  • ✔ See the sea from Ondodai Bridge
  • ✔ Scenery is beautiful and perfect for a mood change

Caution

  • ・There are many traffic lights in the city area
  • ・There are many hills, so physical strength is required
