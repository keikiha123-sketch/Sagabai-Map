What is Sagabai Map?

Sagabai Map is a website designed to help you discover the true charms of Saga City. We provide authentic, local information and hidden spots that you won't find in standard guidebooks.

Each spot includes insights and local perspectives that only those who have actually experienced them can provide, offering visitors a new way to enjoy Saga City.

Use Sagabai Map to make your sightseeing and travel experience in Saga even more fulfilling!

Unique to Sagabai Map

Visitors from outside the prefecture often flock to famous spots like the "Yoshinogari Ruins."
However, this map only features spots and experiences
actually visited by residents living in Saga City.

It is packed with authentic comments from a local perspective,
as well as "activities and scenery created by nature"
and "experiences unique to Saga" that we have carefully selected.

Let's go explore Saga City!
